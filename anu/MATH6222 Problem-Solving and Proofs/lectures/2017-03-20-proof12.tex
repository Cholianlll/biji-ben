\paragraph{Proof:}\ \\

Injection: $S\to 2^{S}$.

$x\to \{x\} (\subseteq S)$\\

Bijection:

Suppose $\exists$ bijection $f:S\to 2^{S}$

We want to produce a subset $T\subseteq S$ such that $T$ is not in the image of $f$.

Define $T$ as follows:

\[x \in T \iff x\not\in f(x)\]

It follow that $ T\not = f(x)$ for any $x\in S$. Done.\\
