\subsection{Variance Inflation Factor (for variable $X_j$)}

In the set $X_1, X_2,\dots, X_k$.

\[\text{VIF}_j=\frac{1}{1-R_j^2}\]

where $R_j^2$ is the $R^2$ for the regression of $X_j$ on $X_1,X_2,\dots,X_{j-1}, X_{j+1},\dots, X_k.$
